How it Works

We will do sentiment analysis to get the emotion behind each sentence in your script. Then, we analyze the sentences in your audio recording to see if your voice emotions match. We score you in two ways: percentage of sentences for which your voice emotion matches the text emotion, and also a proprietary score that measures how close your emotions were to the text's emotions. This proprietary score is out of 10,000 with 10,000 being perfectly matching. Good luck practicing!
